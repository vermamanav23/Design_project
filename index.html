Frequently Asked Questions

what is the goal of devspod community ?

Our motivation is to provide students with free guidance and learning paths for many technologies being used in the industry. Students will also be provided with mentorship by different active community members helping their juniors.

How can i join the community ?

The website is itself provided with links to our socials where anyone interested in learning about software technology can get started by engaging with the community and can start their journey be following any path from provided.

Is this community only for students ?

No, this community is formed with a goal to provide a clear career path to anybody who wants to amke a career in tech,Irrespective of thier education background.

Are thier any programs which help in learning ?

Devspod community promotes varies open source programs that are being held each year providing students with real opportunities to show thier skills. The links for the following are provided in info section of the webpage.
